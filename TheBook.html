The Book

  • Home
  • Artist Statement

The Book is a photo series that explores scale and perspective to push the boundaries of an otherwise mundane world. We, as a species, have established ourselves at the top of the hierarchy of beings. Some believe there is a celestial force or entity that is greater, but nothing within the current realm of knowledge. A 1-story building may be trivial to us, but that same building would seem immeasurable to an ant. The figurine brings the vastness of our universe into perspective. It scales down a world that we have lost appreciation for, allowing the viewer to superimpose themselves onto the figurine and see their existence in a new light.
